Our Training Programs

We offer hands-on, accessible, and industry-aligned programs designed to uplift communities through practical knowledge and career-ready skills.

💻 Digital Literacy

Master the basics of computer operation, internet navigation, email usage, and online safety to confidently participate in the digital world.

🖥️ Web Development

Learn how to build websites using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. From static pages to dynamic apps, unlock the power of the web.

🧰 IT Support & Helpdesk

Gain the skills to troubleshoot hardware, manage networks, and support users — perfect for launching an entry-level IT career.

📱 Mobile Tech & Repairs

Train in mobile phone maintenance, hardware replacement, and common software fixes — great for self-employment and community support.

🎨 Graphic Design & Branding

Learn how to use design tools like Canva and Adobe tools to create stunning visuals, logos, posters, and digital branding materials.

🌍 Freelancing & Digital Income

Understand online work platforms, digital marketing basics, portfolio creation, and how to earn income online — responsibly and sustainably.

Program Highlights

  • ✅ Available in local languages for broader access
  • ✅ Self-paced & live cohort options
  • ✅ Designed for beginners — no prior experience needed
  • ✅ Blended format: online & on-ground workshops
  • ✅ Free for qualifying learners through community sponsorship
